AI-Based Early Cancer Risk Detection System

Medical AI Preventive Medicine Research

Goal

Design an intelligent system to estimate cancer risk early using symptoms and clinical indicators.

Approach

Combined classification, clustering, and decision-tree logic using real medical datasets.

Outcome

A prototype expert system capable of screening risk for several cancer types.

Reflection

This project shaped my interest in preventive medicine and AI-driven diagnosis.

Project Summary

This system was designed to support early cancer screening by analyzing patient symptoms and risk factors before expensive clinical testing. It targets cancers such as breast, lung, stomach, oral, and cervical cancer.

System Capabilities
  • Analyzes patient symptoms and basic clinical indicators
  • Estimates probability of cancer risk using data mining techniques
  • Supports early decision making before lab and imaging tests
  • Reduces cost, time, and diagnostic delay
Why This Matters

Many cancers are detected too late. This project focuses on shifting diagnosis toward prevention and early intervention using accessible technology.
